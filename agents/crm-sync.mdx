Your CRM is only as useful as it is accurate — and manual data entry is the enemy of accuracy. Zig's CRM Sync agent keeps your CRM up to date after every interaction, from a voice command in the car to a post-meeting approval on your phone. You get 95% CRM accuracy without touching a field yourself.

## What the CRM Sync Agent Does

The CRM Sync agent works in two modes: you can tell it what changed and it updates your CRM, or it watches your interactions and suggests updates for you to approve. After every call, email, or meeting, Zig surfaces a set of proposed CRM changes — stage updates, contact field changes, notes, next steps — so you review once and move on instead of hunting through your CRM to find the right fields.

Voice commands make field updates frictionless in the moments when you're most likely to have context but least likely to have your laptop open. Call your Zig number from your car, say "move Acme to Proposal Sent," and the update is logged before you park. No app to open, no field to find, no risk of forgetting by the time you get to your desk.

The agent integrates with Salesforce and other major CRMs, pushing structured updates that respect your existing pipeline stages, custom fields, and data hygiene rules. Your ops team doesn't need to clean up after Zig — the data lands clean.

## How to Use the CRM Sync Agent

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect your CRM">
    Go to **Settings → Integrations → CRM** and authorize your Salesforce or other CRM connection. Map your pipeline stages and confirm field assignments — this takes about five minutes once.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review post-interaction suggestions">
    After every call, meeting, or email thread, open the CRM Sync review card in your Zig dashboard. Zig lists every proposed update with the reason it was suggested. Approve all, approve individually, or edit before saving.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use voice commands on the go">
    Save your Zig number as a contact on your phone. After a meeting, call it from your car and say what changed: "Mark Northstar Corp as Negotiation stage, add a note that they want a security review before signing." Done.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check pipeline insights">
    Open the **Pipeline** tab in your dashboard to see Zig's read on your current deals — which are progressing, which are stalled, and which have revenue signals worth acting on. No report-building required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Audit your CRM health">
    Use the CRM accuracy dashboard to see how many fields are populated, which contacts are missing data, and where Zig has filled gaps. Your CRM admin will thank you.
  </Step>
</Steps>
