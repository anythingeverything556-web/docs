Zig's Research agent turns a single sentence into a fully enriched prospect list — and keeps working in the background so you always know who to talk to next, and exactly why. Whether you're building a pipeline from scratch, scanning a badge at a conference, or following up after a big close, the Research agent gives you the buyer intelligence and account context you need before you say a word.

## What the Research Agent Does

The Research agent starts with your ICP and playbook. Describe your target — industry, role, company size, pain point, whatever matters — and Zig cross-verifies contact and company data across public and private datasets to surface accounts and contacts that match. You get enriched leads complete with role context, company signals, and the right moment to reach out, not just a list of names.

For field reps, the agent includes instant contact scanning: point your phone at a badge or a LinkedIn QR code and research is ready before the conversation starts. You'll know what they do, what their company is going through, and how to open strong.

After you close a deal, the Research agent surfaces lookalike accounts — companies that resemble your newest customer — with drafted outreach already attached. Your momentum doesn't stop at the signature; it accelerates.

## How to Use the Research Agent

<Steps>
  <Step title="Describe your target">
    Type one sentence (or a few keywords) describing your ideal prospect — role, industry, company size, pain point, or any combination. You can also paste a URL, a company name, or a job title.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review your enriched lead list">
    Zig returns a list of ICP-matched contacts enriched with role context, company signals, and outreach timing recommendations. Review, filter, or export the list directly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scan a badge or QR code (field reps)">
    Open Zig on your phone and tap the scan icon. Point at a conference badge or LinkedIn QR code. The Research agent pulls buyer context instantly — no typing required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Activate lookalike discovery after a close">
    Mark a deal as won in your CRM. The Research agent automatically surfaces companies that match your new customer's profile with drafted outreach ready to review.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send to Outreach">
    Select any contact or account and send it directly to the Outreach agent to begin a personalized sequence — no copy-pasting required.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Tips

<Tip>
  The more specific your ICP description, the sharper your lead list. Include firmographic filters like "Series B SaaS companies with a VP of Sales in the Southeast" rather than broad terms like "tech companies."
</Tip>

<Tip>
  Use the lookalike feature intentionally: right after closing a deal, open Zig and review the surfaced accounts while that win is fresh. The context from your recent close makes the drafted outreach feel natural and relevant.
</Tip>
