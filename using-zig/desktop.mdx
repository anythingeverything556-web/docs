The Zig desktop experience puts your entire AI sales operation in one place. From a single surface, you get full visibility into your pipeline, your agents, and every action waiting on you — without juggling tabs, toggling between tools, or losing context mid-deal.

## Your Morning Brief

Open Zig and your day is already organized. The morning brief surfaces before you've had your first cup of coffee: leads ranked by urgency, next steps queued for each open opportunity, and deal alerts flagged overnight by your agents. You don't start the day guessing what matters — Zig tells you.

Every brief adapts to your current pipeline. New inbound leads appear at the top. Deals that have gone quiet get surfaced. Pending approvals waiting on you are called out so nothing slips through.

## Managing Agent Workflows

Zig runs six AI agent workflows — and the desktop is where you oversee all of them at once. You can review what each agent has done, approve queued actions, and trigger new work without leaving the main interface.

## Working from Email and Slack

You don't have to live inside the Zig app for it to work for you. Zig meets you inside the tools you already use every day. When a hot lead replies to an outreach email, that thread surfaces in your Gmail or Outlook with agent context already attached — suggested reply, deal history, next step recommendation — so you can act immediately.

Slack works the same way. When an agent flags something urgent, it drops a message in your configured channel. Reply, assign, or approve right from the thread.

<Note>
  The Zig Slack integration supports full two-way interaction. You can approve actions, query deal status, and trigger agent tasks directly from Slack without opening the Zig app. Ask your workspace admin to connect Slack under **Settings → Integrations**.
</Note>

## Start Your Day with Zig

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check your morning brief">
    Open Zig and read through the brief your agents prepared overnight. Review prioritized leads, deal alerts, and recommended next steps before your first meeting.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review priority leads">
    Work down your ranked lead list. For each high-priority lead, confirm the suggested action — follow-up, outreach, or escalation — and assign it or approve it on the spot.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Approve pending actions">
    Open the approvals queue and process any cards waiting on you. One click to approve, or tap in to edit the draft before it goes out.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check pipeline alerts">
    Scan the pipeline view for deals your agents have flagged — stalled opportunities, stage-change recommendations, or time-sensitive follow-ups that need attention today.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  You can approve actions directly from your email inbox without ever opening the Zig app. Approval notification emails include inline approve, edit, and dismiss buttons — so you stay in flow even when you're deep in your inbox.
</Tip>
