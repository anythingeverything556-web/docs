ZigMobile is built for the way field sales actually works — on the move, between meetings, and away from a desk. Your AI agents travel with you. Every capability available on desktop comes with you into the room: lead research, CRM updates, approvals, and your morning brief. Your desk is wherever you're selling.

## Badge and Card Scanning

Walk into a conference, tradeshow, or client office and turn every badge or business card into a fully researched contact — before the conversation is over. ZigMobile's scanner reads event badges, business cards, and LinkedIn QR codes, then immediately pulls the contact's LinkedIn profile, verified email, phone number, and a full company intelligence brief.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open ZigMobile and tap Scan">
    Launch the app and tap the **Scan** button on your home screen. Point your camera at a badge, business card, or LinkedIn QR code.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Capture the contact">
    ZigMobile reads the contact information and begins pulling enrichment data in real time — LinkedIn profile, job title, company size, recent news, and verified contact details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review the full profile and research">
    In seconds, you have a complete picture of who you're talking to and what their company is doing. Save the contact, log a note, or trigger a follow-up sequence — all before the conversation wraps up.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Voice-Powered CRM Updates

After a meeting, say what happened out loud — ZigMobile captures it and your CRM is updated before you're back at your desk. Speak naturally using Zig's voice commands, and your AI agent handles the rest: logging activities, updating deal stages, and queuing follow-up actions.

For a full list of supported voice commands and how they're processed, see the [Voice Commands](/using-zig/voice-commands) page.

## Morning Brief on Mobile

Your day is organized before your first meeting starts. ZigMobile delivers your morning brief the same way the desktop does — leads ranked by priority, next steps queued, and deal alerts flagged overnight. Open the app on your commute and you already know what the day requires.

Each brief item is actionable. Tap a lead to review the full record, approve a queued follow-up, or call a contact directly from the brief without switching apps.
