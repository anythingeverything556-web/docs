The mission

# To give sales back to the people whose lives it changed

Sales gave a lot of us things we never thought possible. Opportunities our families only dreamed of. Proof that where you started had nothing to do with where you could go. 
Then somewhere along the way, the joy of it got lost. The craft got buried. The people who were built for this started spending their best hours on everything except the reason they fell in love with it. 
That's why we built Zig. For them.

Our vision

## A world where people love what they do.

Work was never supposed to feel like a burden. The best version of any profession looks more like a craft than a job. Something that gives you energy, identity, and purpose.

## Zig starts in sales. But the vision is bigger.

Brilliant people exist in every industry. And in every industry, there's a weight between them and the work that lights them up. Slowly pulling them away from the reason they started. 
We're building the infrastructure that removes that weight permanently. So careers become callings. Jobs become crafts. And the work that once felt like a chore feels like the reason you get up in the morning. 
That's the world Zig is building toward.

Our story

## When Sellers met Builders

Zig didn't start in a boardroom. It started with three people who had each solved half of the hardest problem in sales.

Steve had spent 16 years in the trenches — carrying quota, leading teams, and building systems to fix the execution gaps that were costing companies revenue and burning out the people they hired to generate it.

Lavish and Piyush had spent over a decade building machine learning infrastructure with intelligence at its core.

Not automation bolted on top. Systems that understood context, learned from behavior, and adapted as outcomes unfolded. Deep architecture that most sales tools had never attempted.

![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/692db0eaf3c473ac91a06392/699834a7d365eac036d2a6b1_Rectangle%201.webp)![](https://cdn.prod.website-files.com/692db0eaf3c473ac91a06392/69984a2aab7f9709a6a82b02_photo__mob-1.webp)

When the three of them connected, it was obvious.

## Two teams. 
Two breakthroughs. 
One missing piece each.

Steve brought the domain truth. Lavish and Piyush brought the technical depth to match it. Together, they had something the market had never seen — a system that could think and move at the same time.
