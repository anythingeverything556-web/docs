Before there were dashboards, intent signals, and automated sequences, there were maps, territories, and doors to knock on.

Zig founder and CEO [Steve Ancheta](https://www.linkedin.com/in/steve-ancheta-b9615541/) started his sales career the hard way -  commission-only, door-to-door, in person.

And it's that foundation, not Silicon Valley orthodoxy, that shapes how he thinks about where AI fits in modern selling.

His latest piece in _CRM Magazine_ (published by destinationCRM.com) makes the case that the core lessons from classic door-to-door selling offer a clearer blueprint for building agentic AI than most of what's being published in the space today.

**Read the full piece on destinationCRM.com >** [What Door-to-Door Sales Can Teach Us About Building Agentic AI for Sales Teams](https://www.destinationcrm.com/Articles/Web-Exclusives/Viewpoints/What-Door-to-Door-Sales-Can-Teach-Us-About-Building-Agentic-AI-for-Sales-Teams-174312.aspx)
