Steve Ancheta, Founder and CEO at Zig, recently joined Fred Davis on _The Power Connect_ — a podcast covering the intersection of technology, business, and the people building what's next.

The conversation went wide: from the founding story behind Zig, to bold predictions about where AI infrastructure is headed, to why the relationship is becoming the last true differentiator in sales.

## **Why this episode is worth your time**

A few highlights from their conversation:

- **Domain expertise is the only moat left:** Steve breaks down why the companies that will win in AI are the ones that combine deep domain knowledge with their own data, workflows, and intelligence.
- **The compounding context layer:** Zig doesn't just learn what your team does. It learns how they do it, at both the org level and the individual rep level.
- **The AI volume backlash:** Mass email and LinkedIn outreach are already desensitizing buyers. Steve talks about why relationship is becoming the last real differentiator.
- **Why Zig sits in the middle:** Generic AI chat tools are too loose. Legacy SaaS is too rigid. Steve explains the gap Zig fills.
- **Advice for founders:** Steve's take on velocity, getting to market fast, and why the biggest mistake founders make is building in a lab instead of in front of customers.
