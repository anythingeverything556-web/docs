**San Francisco, CA - March 10, 2026 —** Today, [Zig.ai](https://zig.ai) announces the official launch of its AI sales execution platform, introducing a pricing model that is designed to replace traditional per-seat software licensing with outcome-based billing.

Founded by [Steve Ancheta](https://www.linkedin.com/in/steve-ancheta-b9615541/), Zig.ai embeds AI agents directly into sales and operational workflows, enabling companies to automate execution across revenue teams while learning from every interaction, conversation, and signal. Over time, the data compounds into an intelligence layer that builds organizational memory and improves sales efficiency.

> _Know more about how Zig.ai creates compounding intelligence for your organization_ [_here_](https://zig.ai/platform/)_._

“For the last two decades, enterprise software has been stuck in a contradiction,” says Ancheta, founder & CEO of Zig.ai. “Organizations want a single place to command their entire departmental strategy, but software limitations have made it nearly impossible. Because Zig.ai is built as an AI infrastructure at its core, it gives us the flexibility to create a dynamic experience that continuously learns and improves how companies execute their revenue operations.”

Traditional sales platforms typically charge per user, creating what many operators describe as “license bloat,” or underutilized seats that inflate costs without clear return on investment. Zig.ai eliminates per-seat pricing entirely and instead deploys AI agents to execute defined tasks within revenue workflows. Each action is verified through system logs, and customers are billed only when outcomes are successfully delivered. 

**Zig.ai officially launched in January 2026 and is currently working with companies across high-volume sales teams.**

**The company raised $3 million in funding led by** [**super{set}**](https://superset.com/)**, a data and AI-focused venture studio based in San Francisco.**

> “Zig.ai stands out because it’s being built by people who have actually lived the problem,” _says Peter Day, General Partner at super{set}_. “The team has a deep understanding of the pain points inside sales organizations. We invested in Zig.ai because they’re not just building AI features, but rethinking how enterprise software creates and captures value. With every iteration, the platform gets smarter and more refined, and customers genuinely love using it.”

## About Zig.ai

Zig.ai is an AI-powered sales execution platform that embeds AI agents into revenue workflows. The platform captures data from every interaction, allowing intelligence to compound over time and continuously improve how sales teams execute. The company replaces traditional per-seat licensing with outcome-based pricing, charging customers only for verified work completed by AI agents. Zig.ai is headquartered in San Francisco.
