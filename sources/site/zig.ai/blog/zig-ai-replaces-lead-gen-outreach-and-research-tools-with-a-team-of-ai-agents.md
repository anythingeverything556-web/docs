# Zig.ai Launches Feature That Replaces Lead Gen, Outreach and Research Tools With a Team of AI Agents

Zig.ai launches AI agents that autonomously handle lead generation, research, and outreach—replacing separate tools and eliminating 2-4 hours of manual work per rep. When reps close deals, the agents analyze patterns and surface lookalike accounts with drafted outreach sequences ready to trigger.

‍[**Zig.ai**](https://zig.ai) today brings an AI agent team to its fully autonomous sales platform, adding automated lead generation, research and outreach to a system built to run every stage of a rep’s sales motion.

> **_"We built Zig.ai to give every account executive their own team of AI executive assistants, so reps only have to worry about building relationships and attending customer calls.” — Steve Ancheta, CEO of Zig.ai_**

Where reps once managed separate tools for every step of prospecting, Zig.ai gives every account executive their own AI assistants that handle the actions autonomously. Reps describe the target. The agents handle the rest.

From finding prospects and qualifying leads to researching companies, the agents complete in minutes what once took reps two to four hours.

"Sales reps have always needed their own employees that could handle the majority of their workload," says [**Steve Ancheta**](https://www.linkedin.com/in/steve-ancheta-b9615541/), founder and CEO of Zig.ai. “We built Zig.ai to give every account executive their own team of AI executive assistants, so reps only have to worry about building relationships and attending customer calls.”

Unlike traditional lead generation platforms that pull from a single data source, Zig.ai cross-verifies contact and company data across both public and private datasets, surfacing verified emails, phone numbers and contact details that have been validated across multiple providers.

The launch also introduces lookalike account intelligence. When a sales rep closes a deal, Zig.ai's agents analyze the patterns within that closed deal and surface a list of similar companies, complete with a drafted outreach sequence ready for the rep to review and trigger.

"Reps don't need another tool," says [**Lavish Mantri**](https://www.linkedin.com/in/lavish-mantri/), co-founder and head of engineering at Zig.ai. "They need their best deals to teach the system. Every close sharpens who we go after, what we say, when we send. Reps focus on winning the deal. The agents make closing a repeatable motion.”

The new feature is the latest addition to Zig.ai’s growing AI agent team, which previously launched [**Zigscribe**](https://zig.ai/blog/zig-ai-launches-zigscribe-the-ai-employee-that-turns-sales-meetings-into-action), an AI employee that turns sales meetings into execution.

**About Zig.ai**
