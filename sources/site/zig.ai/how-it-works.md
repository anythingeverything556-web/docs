## FAQ

Something we didn't cover? Talk to us.

I already use Salesforce, Gong, and Apollo. Do I have to give them up?

No. Your assistants connect to the tools you already use — CRM, inbox, calendar, outreach platforms. Zig reads what's there and starts working from day one. No migration, no rip-and-replace.

Can I use Zig from my phone or by voice?

Yes. The ZigMobile app lets you command your assistants from anywhere — scan a badge, send a voice update, approve an outreach sequence. Your team travels with you.

How quickly does it start doing useful work?

Most reps see their first output — a lead list, a morning brief, a drafted follow-up — within their first session. The more your assistants learn about how you sell, the sharper they get.

My reps won't adopt another tool. Why is this different?

Because they don't have to open it. Zig works through the surfaces your reps already use — email, Slack, their phone. The assistants do the work in the background. Reps review and approve, they don't operate.

Do my reps send things manually, or does Zig act on its own?

Your reps stay in control. Zig drafts, prepares, and proposes — your reps approve before anything goes out. One tap, one sentence, back to selling.

How does Zig get smarter over time?

Every call, email, and deal adds to what your assistants know about your team, your buyers, and what closes. At month 12, they know your pipeline better than a new hire could in their first year. That's the compounding effect — and it's yours.

Who is Zig built for?

Any rep who spends too much time on work that isn't selling. Any leader who's tired of pipeline reviews that don't reflect reality. From a solo founder doing their own outreach to a VP running a team of fifty.

How does Zig price — is it per seat?

No per-seat charges. You pre-buy a pool of executions — the work your assistants actually do. Unused executions carry forward. You pay for output, not headcount.
