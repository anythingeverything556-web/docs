Zig is an AI sales execution platform that gives every rep their own team of AI Executive Assistants. Instead of spending your day logging calls, writing follow-up emails, updating pipeline fields, and switching between eight different tools, you focus on selling — and Zig handles everything else automatically, in the background, across every deal you're running.

## The Execution Gap

The modern sales process doesn't have a strategy problem — it has an execution problem. After every customer call, a rep faces 40 minutes of work that no one built a dedicated tool for: updating CRM fields, writing follow-up emails, scheduling next steps, researching the next account on the list. Multiply that across a full week and you lose entire days to work that doesn't directly move deals forward.

<Note>
  Research shows most sales reps spend only **28–36% of their day actually selling**. The rest goes to administrative tasks, tool-switching, and manual follow-through that should be automated.
</Note>

Zig was built to close that gap. It sits on top of the tools you already use — your CRM, inbox, calendar, and Slack — and executes the work you don't have time for, at the speed and consistency no human team can match.

## How Zig Gets Smarter

Zig doesn't just automate tasks — it builds compounding revenue intelligence from every interaction across your entire sales org.

Every call ZigScribe listens to, every email Zig drafts, and every deal it tracks feeds a shared intelligence layer that makes every future action sharper. Here's how that compounds:

- **Month 1** — Zig learns your CRM structure, open deals, and ideal customer profiles. It begins executing with high accuracy from day one.
- **Month 3** — Zig starts spotting patterns across your best reps: which talk tracks, sequences, and follow-up timing lead to closed-won deals.
- **Month 4** — Zig flags deal risk proactively — surfacing accounts that are going quiet or have stalled before you notice it in your pipeline.
- **Month 6** — Zig knows more about your accounts, your buyers, and your winning patterns than any new hire you could onboard.

The longer Zig runs, the more valuable it becomes — not just faster execution, but sharper judgment built from real data inside your own revenue motion.
