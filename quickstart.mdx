Follow this guide to go from zero to your first AI-executed sales action. By the end, you'll have your CRM connected, your inbox linked, and Zig ready to research, draft, and log on your behalf — without you opening a new tab to do any of it.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Book your onboarding session">
    Visit [zig.ai/book-a-meeting](https://zig.ai/book-a-meeting) to schedule a live setup session with the Zig onboarding team. They'll walk you through connecting your tools, configuring your first agent workflows, and making sure Zig is calibrated to your sales motion from day one.

    <Tip>
      On the **Solo Pro** plan? You can self-onboard entirely in-app. Connect your CRM, follow the setup prompts, and Zig will guide you through each step without a call.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your CRM">
    Link Salesforce or your supported CRM from the Zig integrations panel. Zig will immediately import your open deals, contacts, and ideal customer profiles.

    From that point forward, every interaction Zig handles — every call note, every follow-up, every next step — flows back into your CRM automatically. You get **95% CRM accuracy** with zero manual entry required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your inbox and calendar">
    Link Gmail or Outlook so Zig can draft follow-up emails in your voice and send meeting prep briefs before every scheduled call. Connect Google Calendar or Outlook Calendar so Zig knows what's on your schedule and can prepare you automatically.

    Zig reads your writing style and communication history to make sure every draft sounds like you — not a template.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Slack (optional)">
    Install the Zig Slack app from your workspace settings. Once connected, Zig surfaces approval cards directly inside Slack — so you can review, edit, and approve AI-drafted actions without switching to another tool or opening a new tab.

    Zig meets you where you already work. If your team lives in Slack, your entire approval flow can live there too.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Approve your first AI action">
    After your first meeting or prospecting run, Zig will surface an approval card — a drafted follow-up email, a CRM update, or a set of researched talking points. Review the output, make any edits you want, and tap once to approve.

    That's the entire workflow. Zig does the work. You stay in control with a single tap.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Most reps see their first Zig output within **24 hours** of connecting their CRM — often before their next scheduled meeting.
</Note>

## What Happens Next

Once your tools are connected and your first action is approved, Zig starts compounding. It learns which accounts you engage most, which follow-up patterns get replies, and which deals are moving versus stalling. By month three, Zig is spotting which deals close and which go quiet — before you notice. By month six, it knows your pipeline better than any new hire you could bring on.

You don't need to configure anything for this to happen. Every call ZigScribe listens to and every email Zig drafts feeds the intelligence layer automatically.
