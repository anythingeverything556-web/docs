The Zig Slack integration brings your entire sales execution workflow into the tool your team already lives in. Instead of switching to the Zig app to approve a follow-up, check your morning brief, or act on a hot lead, you receive interactive cards directly in Slack and handle everything with a single tap. Your whole team can stay on top of pipeline activity, respond to inbound signals, and keep deals moving — all without opening another tab.

## How to Install

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Integrations Settings">
    Navigate to **Settings** in the Zig app and select the **Integrations** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect to Slack">
    Find **Slack** in the integrations list and click **Connect to Slack**. You'll be redirected to Slack's authorization page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize the Zig App">
    Select the Slack workspace where you want to install Zig and click **Allow**. Once authorized, you'll return to Zig and the integration will be active. Zig will begin delivering cards and alerts to the channels and users you configure.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Approving Actions from Slack

When Zig drafts an action — whether it's a follow-up email, a CRM field update, or a meeting summary — an interactive card appears in your configured Slack channel or DM. Each card includes a preview of the proposed action alongside three response options:

- **Approve** — Zig executes the action immediately using the draft as shown.
- **Edit** — Opens a lightweight editor so you can adjust the content before it goes out.
- **Dismiss** — Cancels the action without any changes being made.

No context-switching, no extra logins. The card contains everything you need to make the call.

<Tip>
  Your whole team can work entirely from Slack on high-volume days — approving actions, reviewing briefs, and responding to leads — without ever opening the Zig app. Set up dedicated channels for pipeline alerts and approvals to keep the signal clean.
</Tip>

<Note>
  The Slack integration respects the same approval rules configured in the main Zig app. If a field is set to require manual approval before writing to your CRM, that requirement applies to approvals made from Slack as well.
</Note>
