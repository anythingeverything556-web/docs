Connecting your email and calendar gives Zig everything it needs to operate as a true execution layer for your outbound and follow-up workflow. Zig drafts follow-up emails in your voice and sends them from your actual address, generates meeting prep briefs pulled directly from your calendar events, and attaches ZigScribe to your live meetings for real-time transcription — all without you switching between tools or writing a single message from scratch.

## Supported Providers

- **Email:** Gmail, Outlook (Microsoft 365)
- **Calendar:** Google Calendar, Microsoft Calendar

## How to Connect

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Email & Calendar Settings">
    Go to **Settings** in the Zig app and select the **Email & Calendar** tab.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Your Provider">
    Select **Gmail** or **Outlook** depending on which platform your organization uses. You can connect both an email account and a calendar account in the same step if they share the same provider.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize Access">
    You'll be redirected to Google or Microsoft to complete authorization. Grant Zig permission to read your calendar events and send emails on your behalf. Once authorized, you'll be returned to Zig and your connection will be active immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What Email Connection Unlocks

- **AI-drafted follow-ups in your voice** — after every meeting or call, Zig drafts a personalized follow-up email that sounds like you. Review it, approve it, and it goes out from your address.
- **Outreach sequences sent from your account** — when Zig runs outbound sequences on your behalf, every email is sent through your connected inbox using your domain. Recipients see your name, not Zig's.
- **Smart silence detection** — Zig monitors thread activity to detect when a prospect has gone quiet and surfaces those threads for timely re-engagement, so no deal slips through inactivity.

## What Calendar Connection Unlocks

- **Auto-generated meeting prep briefs** — before each calendar event, Zig compiles a brief covering the attendee's background, recent activity, open deals, and suggested talk tracks so you walk into every meeting prepared.
- **ZigScribe live transcription** — ZigScribe automatically attaches to your calendar meetings to capture and transcribe conversations in real time, so you can focus on the conversation instead of taking notes.
- **Calendar-aware scheduling** — use voice commands like "Offer her times from my calendar" and Zig pulls your real availability to suggest meeting slots without you opening a scheduling tool.

<Note>
  All outbound emails are sent from your actual email address. Recipients see your name and your domain in every message — not a Zig address. This keeps your sender reputation intact and your outreach feeling personal.
</Note>

<Tip>
  Connect both your email and your calendar at the same time to unlock the full ZigScribe experience. ZigScribe needs your calendar connection to detect upcoming meetings and attach automatically — without it, you'll need to start transcription manually for each session.
</Tip>
