Zig is priced on what actually matters: the volume of AI execution work done on your behalf. You're not paying per seat, per token, or per feature flag — you're sizing your plan to the workload Zig absorbs for your team. That means every user in your organization gets access to the full platform on day one, and the plan you choose reflects the pipeline throughput you need, not the number of people on your roster.

## How Executions Work

Zig execution pools are designed to flex with your business. Unused credits and minutes carry forward from month to month, so a slow week doesn't mean wasted spend. If your team runs a campaign blitz or a big event drives a spike in pipeline, Zig absorbs temporary volume increases without requiring a plan change. When higher throughput becomes your new baseline — not just a spike — that's when a conversation about right-sizing your plan makes sense.

- **Unused executions carry forward** — nothing expires at the end of the month
- **Temporary spikes are absorbed** — campaign bursts won't break your limits
- **Sustainable growth becomes a conversation** — not a surprise overage charge

## Frequently Asked Questions

<Accordion title="Why isn't Zig priced per seat?">
  Per-seat pricing charges you for headcount, not for outcomes. Zig's value is measured in the AI execution work it absorbs — emails drafted, CRM records updated, calls transcribed, leads qualified. Sizing by workload means you pay for what Zig actually does, and adding a new rep to your team doesn't automatically increase your bill.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What's included in every plan?">
  Every Zig plan includes full platform access with no feature gates. ZigScribe, ZigDialer, ZigLead, AI outbound sequences, CRM sync, voice commands, Slack integration, and email/calendar connection are all available on every tier. New features ship to every plan automatically — there's no waiting for a higher tier to unlock something new.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What happens as my team grows?">
  Adding team members doesn't change your pricing on Starter Team, Growth Team, or Enterprise — users are unlimited. If your execution workload grows sustainably (more emails, more calls, more leads), that's a signal to right-size your plan. Zig will flag it and have a conversation with you rather than silently billing overages.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Is there a free trial?">
  Zig doesn't offer a time-boxed free trial, but it does offer a **Proof of Value** engagement — a structured evaluation designed to demonstrate measurable pipeline impact before you make a long-term commitment. [Book your session here](https://zig.ai/book-a-meeting).
</Accordion>
