Every Zig plan comes with a built-in execution pool sized for your team's typical workload — but when you need more reach, more capacity, or a new channel, add-ons let you extend your plan without upgrading it. Whether you're adding an inbound qualification layer, running a calling-heavy campaign, or expanding into multi-channel outreach, Zig's add-ons are designed to fit alongside your existing plan with no long-term lock-in.

## AI Agent Inbound Engine

The AI Agent Inbound Engine transforms your inbound funnel into an automated pipeline. It monitors your web traffic and ad campaigns for real-time intent signals, qualifies every inbound lead using AI, and routes ready buyers directly to the right rep or sequence — all without manual intervention. When intent fires, follow-up goes out immediately.

## Top-Up Add-Ons

Need more of a specific resource without changing your base plan? Top-up add-ons let you purchase additional credits, minutes, and hours on a month-to-month basis.

| Add-On | What You Get | Price |
|---|---|---|
| **Enrichment Boost** | +500 ZigLead credits | $30/mo |
| **Meetings Boost** | +20 ZigScribe recording hours | $30/mo |
| **Calling Boost** | +400 ZigDialer minutes | $40/mo |
| **Sequences Boost** | +500 outreach contacts | $60/mo |
| **Multi-Channel** *(Solo Pro only)* | WhatsApp, SMS, and LinkedIn outreach | $60/mo |

<Tip>
  Add-ons are purchased month-to-month and don't extend or alter the term of your base plan. You can add or remove them at any time — they're designed for flexibility, not commitment.
</Tip>

## Frequently Asked Questions

<Accordion title="When would I need the AI Agent Inbound Engine?">
  The AI Agent Inbound Engine is most valuable when you have significant inbound traffic — from content, SEO, paid ads, or brand awareness — and want AI to handle the first layer of qualification and routing automatically. If leads currently sit in a queue waiting for a rep to review and respond, the Inbound Engine compresses that window to near-zero and ensures every ready buyer gets a personalized follow-up the moment they show intent.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Can I add the Multi-Channel add-on to a team plan?">
  The Multi-Channel add-on (WhatsApp, SMS, and LinkedIn) is currently available for **Solo Pro only**. Team plans handle LinkedIn outreach separately — Growth Team includes 2 LinkedIn outreach accounts as part of the base plan, and Enterprise can configure LinkedIn at custom volume. If WhatsApp and SMS are important for your team workflow, reach out to your Zig account team to discuss options.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="What happens to unused top-up credits?">
  Unused credits and minutes from top-up add-ons follow the same carry-forward rules as your base plan allocation. They roll over from month to month and don't expire at the end of a billing cycle, so a month where you purchase a boost but don't use it all isn't wasted spend.
</Accordion>
