## Get Up and Running

<Steps>
  <Step title="Connect your CRM">
    Link Salesforce or your supported CRM so Zig can import your deals, contacts, and ICPs on day one.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up your first agent workflow">
    Choose which AI agent to activate first — most reps start with Research or Follow-Up to see immediate time savings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Approve your first AI action">
    After your first meeting or prospecting run, review the AI-drafted action Zig surfaces and tap once to approve.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Watch Zig compound over time">
    As Zig learns from every call, email, and deal, its outputs get sharper — surfacing deal risk, lookalike accounts, and rep patterns automatically.
  </Step>
</Steps>
